## Findings

1. **Launch batch creates a measurement cold-start.** 51 posts were published in 3 weeks
   (2026-06-08 to 2026-06-25), overwhelming the 28-day cadence target of 13 posts. Every metric
   in this report is a baseline reading, not a trend. No threshold (T1–T8) can fire meaningfully
   until at least one additional 28-day window of post-launch data is available and GSC credentials
   are live.

2. **Hardware comparison content leads observable traffic by a 6.7× margin.** `heltec-vs-rak-for-meshcore`
   generated 101 visits in the 7-day window — 6.7× more than the next post (`every-node-heard-it-twice`,
   15 visits). Dispatch/hardware comparison formats are the blog's early traffic leaders. This aligns
   with EDITORIAL.md's note that `dispatch` (comparisons, buying guides) have high commercial intent.

3. **North-star referral rate is 0.24% (7d partial).** Of 2,117 blog visits in 7 days, only 5
   converted to waev.app visits. This is an expected baseline for a blog that launched 3 weeks ago
   with no established organic search traffic. The absolute number should be tracked each cycle; a
   persistent delta ≤0% while clicks grow ≥20% would trigger T6.

4. **Cloudflare zone plan retention blocks the core measurement spec.** The zone at `cf_zone_tag`
   retains `httpRequestsAdaptiveGroups` data for only ~8 days. The 28-day north-star and blog-traffic
   windows defined in MEASUREMENT.md are structurally inaccessible on the current plan. This affects
   every future report until the plan is upgraded or the measurement approach is revised. The referrer
   filter itself (clientRefererHost) is confirmed functional.

5. **GSC credential is misconfigured — four metrics are permanently null until fixed.** The `GSC_SERVICE_ACCOUNT_JSON`
   secret contains a raw PEM private key (1,675 chars starting with the PKCS8 header), not the full
   service account JSON object. Impressions, clicks, CTR, and query-level data (metrics 1–4) are
   entirely inaccessible. Without GSC, thresholds T1–T4 (striking-distance, snippet-fix, decay-refresh,
   position-regression) cannot fire. This is the single highest-priority credential fix.

6. **Post frontmatter lacks editorial metadata — T8 cannot be evaluated.** The 55 blog posts use only
   title/description/date/tags in frontmatter. The editorial rails (bucket/segment/theme/funnel_stage)
   are tracked in calendar.yaml for 8 entries only. Without this metadata in the posts themselves,
   the rolling-quarter mix check (T8) cannot run programmatically. Adding these fields to the post
   frontmatter schema would unblock automated editorial mix monitoring and per-segment performance
   reporting.

   MEASUREMENT.md-specified 28-day north-star and blog-traffic windows. Either upgrade the zone to

   `funnel_stage` as optional fields to the Astro content collection schema. Backfill values for

   Propose as a content-schema PR via `./seo-auditor.md` or as a direct schema PR.
   Route to: `./briefs/seo-auditor.md` (schema change) or direct PR.
