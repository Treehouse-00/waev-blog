# Hero Image Concepts — the anti-rehash ledger

The hero image is the final human handoff before a post publishes (CHARTER gate
2): the **content-writer writes the prompt; a human generates the image.** This
file exists so that across 156 posts a year, no two heroes rehash the same idea.
The writer READS this ledger before writing a prompt and APPENDS its new concept
after. Treat it as canon for "what has already been done."

## House style (medium & mood — never a fixed motif)
The look is consistent; the *subjects* must not be. Hold the style, vary the
idea.
- **Medium:** hand-painted / warm flat illustration with painterly texture and
  soft light. Not photographic, not 3D-render, not corporate-vector, not neon.
- **Mood:** calm, grounded, human, with a little quiet wonder. Confident, not
  loud.
- **Palette:** warm and earthy with one restrained accent. Avoid tech-neon and
  high-saturation glow.
- **Composition:** ONE clear subject or metaphor with breathing room; it must
  read at thumbnail size. No busy collages, no UI screenshots.
- **Concept:** one idea per image; prefer a metaphor for the post's `theme` over
  a literal depiction of the topic.

## Do NOT prescribe these (style guardrails)
- **No "glowing mesh accents."** Glowing mesh nodes / wireframe-topology overlays
  are not part of the house style and must not be defaulted into prompts.
- No literal dashboards, charts, maps-on-screens, or app UI as the subject.

## Saturated motifs — AVOID repeating (the seeded corpus already overuses these)
Across the first eight posts these recur and are now off-limits unless a slot has
a genuinely new take:
- mountain / hill / hilltop landscapes
- night / starry skies, sunsets
- a lone figure holding a handheld radio
- a folding-table / desk "operating position"
- glowing mesh nodes or wireframe overlays

When in doubt, go where the corpus has NOT: extreme close-ups (a connector, a
solder joint, a knob, a hand), interiors and workbenches, everyday-object still
lifes, abstract or diagrammatic-as-art, weather and seasons, aerial / plan
views, unexpected scale or vantage.
